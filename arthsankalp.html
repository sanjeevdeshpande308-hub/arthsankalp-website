What is SIP? Systematic Investment Plan

📈

Rupee Cost Averaging

Invest fixed amount regularly. Buy more units when market is low, fewer when high. Average cost over time.

⚖️

Disciplined Investing

Forces you to invest regularly regardless of market conditions. Removes emotional decision making.

💎

Power of Compounding

Your returns generate more returns. Small regular investments grow into substantial wealth over time.

⏰

Flexible & Affordable

Start with just ₹500/month. Pause, increase, or stop anytime. Perfect for salaried individuals.
